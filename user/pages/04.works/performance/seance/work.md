**Séance - Performance with Erin Womack, featuring Mikey McParlane and Veronica Bleaus | 2013**

Séance is a collaboration between Erin Womack and Scott Andrew featuring Mikey McParlance and Veronica Bleaus. Scott and Erin's characters serve as reptilian mediums channeling the ghostly presence of Mikey and Veronica from Chicago via a google hangout projection of a real-time remote performance. The physical performance was held at Bunker Project as part of Curdled, a Día de los Muertos event.
