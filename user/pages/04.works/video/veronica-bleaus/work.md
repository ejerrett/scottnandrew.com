Scott Andrew and Veronica Bleaus have collaborated for many years. Scott is currently serving as the artistic director for an upcoming stage performance featuring Veronica. Veronica has starred in several of Scott’s video projects, such as Neráides, Feeling Good, and Planet Dragulon. Scott has also done live video in conjunction with Veronica’s performances at AIR, Hothouse, Open Thread events, and they have performed together with the Drift on the three rivers of pittsburgh and at Bunker project. Look for a full length stage show by Veronica and Scott in the near future.
