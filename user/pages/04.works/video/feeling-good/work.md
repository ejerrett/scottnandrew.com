**Feeling Good single channel video | 2011**

Feeling Good is an animated music video by Scott Andrew, featuring Veronica Bleaus and Dani Lamorte. Paper narwhals, dragonflies and electric star glow fill this cartoon world as live action drag queens perform to the song 'Feeling Good' by Nina Simone.
